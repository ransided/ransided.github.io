Ransided
(Ransided)
About

Ransided is a band based out of Joplin, Missouri. They play an eclectic blend of Rock, Punk, and Metal, all with a bit of Jazz-influence. Ransided consists of guitarist, singer, writer, and composer Anika Adame; drummer Edan Adame; and Nathan Little, a bassist and fellow Joplin-based musician.

Ransided was formed in 2022 by Anika as a way to spread songs that she had written and composed. Her brother, Edan, joined her to add drums to her songs. After moving back to the Joplin area, Anika and Edan met Nathan at an open mic night; shortly after, Nathan joined the band.

Ransided has played gigs around Joplin and are actively searching for more opportunities in and out of Joplin, no matter how big or small.
